CANADA START-UP VISA PROGRAM

Canada Startup Visa is an easy entry into the Business ecosystem of North America. This visa program offers easy access to Permanent Residency in Canada to start-up founders, investors & their immediate families

Apply Now

What we offer

Assistance in securing Permanent Residency through our Letter of Support

Permanent Residency can be availed by 3 Founders / Investors

Flexi Office Space for 6 Months in the heart of Toronto

Access to Mentors & Investors in Canada for 6 months

Access to Networking Meets / Events in Canada for 6 months

Credits worth CAD 9,50,000 in partner services (Accounts, Marketing etc)

Additional benefits

Digital Media Tax Credit

CAD 10k Grant for hiring local interns

Flexible Payments

Refundable payment incase of application rejection by Govt of Canada

65% of expenses refundable for Research & Development (for select startups only)

Who Can Apply

  • Entrepreneurs
  • Investors
  • Personal

More than 10% stake in the start-up

Collectively all founders should own more than 51% decision making rights

Innovative Business that can add value to Canada

Control of IP/Assets within the business

Business having an annual revenue of more than INR 60 Lacs will be preferred

However, initial stage start-ups having patents/ IPs can also apply

An annual income of more than INR 25 Lacs

Intent to invest in start-up or an existing investment in a start-up / portfolio of start-ups

If already an investor, should have more than 10% stake in the start-up

Bachelors / Masters degree from a recognized university

Savings or Fixed Deposit of more than INR 10 Lacs

English Proficiency (Benchmark 5 IELTS)

Required For Application
